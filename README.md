#  🧠📧 Mailvora
# 📧 Agentic Email & Communication Tracker
### AI-powered assistant to manage emails, tasks, and follow-ups intelligently

---

## 🧩 Problem Statement
Managing emails is overwhelming, especially when important tasks, deadlines, and follow-ups are buried inside long threads. Users often miss critical actions, forget to respond, or fail to track ongoing conversations effectively. This leads to reduced productivity and missed opportunities in both professional and personal communication.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution
We are building an AI-powered Email & Communication Tracker that acts like a personal executive assistant. The system automatically processes email data, classifies threads, extracts actionable insights, and provides a structured overview of communication.

The solution will:
- Automatically classify email threads into categories such as action-required, informational, meeting-related, etc.
- Extract action items including tasks, deadlines, and responsible persons
- Detect pending follow-ups and unanswered emails
- Generate a prioritized daily digest summarizing important communications
- Enable natural language-based search across emails

This system reduces cognitive load and ensures that no important communication is missed.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Reduce email overload and clutter
- Improve productivity through intelligent task tracking
- Ensure timely follow-ups and responses
- Provide meaningful daily summaries for better decision-making
